Rochester Museum and Science Center

The purpose of the Rochester Museum and Science Center is to fully delve in and explore the curious nature of mankind. From hands-on activities to thoughtful critiques on futuristic developments, The Rochester Museum and Science Center does well to engage an audience keen on experimentation.

Now, if over 1.2 million objects don't seem to be that big of a deal, and the thought of electricity, chemicals, and mechanical know-how doesn't spark a flame, worry not, for there's still more to discover! With an entire planetarium to control at a whim, the Rochester Museum and Science Center even hold light show nights in which the artificial sky lights up with lasers at the sound of Pink Floyd playing in the background.

If none of that sounded fun, than maybe the RMSC really isn't for you. But don't worry, there's plenty more to discover on a tour through Rochester!
