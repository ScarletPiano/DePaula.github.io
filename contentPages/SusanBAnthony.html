Susan B. Anthony Museum

Located in her home, and the site of her arrest for voting in 1872, the Susan B. Anthony Museum and House aims to memorialize the woman who claimed equality for women everywhere. By visiting where she lived, fought, and died, perhaps some of her passion will inspire you to do great things as well, and keep fighting for justice everywhere.
